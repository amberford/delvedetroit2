Detroit's Golden Age

This was a time of great economic growth in America and this time period was especially good to Detroit's economy. Fueled by the giant success in the Auto Industry Detroit was known the world over as an economic power. This spurred the building of new office buildings, cultural buildings, entertainment and cultural centers, as well as major shopping centers.
